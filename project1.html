La Draga, a Neolithic lakeside village

La Draga

“A Neolithic lakeside village”

Introduction Findings
This website was created to ensure proper introduction to a truly unique Neolithic Catalonian site. The site is located on the eastern shore of the Lake Banyoles, in Catalonia. It was discovered in April 1990, result of a project to turn the area into a park. It is dated at the end of the 6th millennium BC. Importance for the Iberian Peninsula ; one of the oldest settlements of agriculture and livestock.
